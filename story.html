Our Story

How we met nine years ago and our journey to date

In the summer of 2012, Knopfler and Beatrix moved to Calgary from Toronto and Vancouver respectively. Both just finished University and thought that moving to Calgary would be a great step for new job opportunities.

In August, Knopfler started working at Enterprise Rent-a-car as a management trainee and Beatrix soon started a month later at the same location.

A month into working with each other, Beatrix made a friendly wager with Knopfler. They would guess how many clients they would interact with that day, Knopfler guessing more than 30, Beatrix guessing 30 or less.

Beatrix lost and ended up treating Knopfler that very night to dinner at an Indian buffet, which they now considered their "first date."

Fast forward nine years later, Beatrix and Knopfler adopted a cat named Percy, moved to London, UK, visted dozens of different countires with many more planned, met each others familes and friends and are ready to tie the knot!
